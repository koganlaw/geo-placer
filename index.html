Geo Placer

You're shown a name. Tap where it belongs on a blank map. The closer you land, the more you score.

How to play

  1. You'll be given a place name — a state, country, or capital — one at a time.
  2. Click the map where you think it is.
  3. Land inside it → a perfect 100. Miss, and points fall off with distance.
  4. Pick a map, length, and mode below, then go. Random order every time.
