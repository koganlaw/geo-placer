Geo Placer

You're shown a name. Tap where it belongs on a blank map. The closer you land, the more you score.

How to play

  1. You'll be given a state name, one at a time.
  2. Click the map where you think that state is.
  3. Land inside the state → a perfect 100. Miss it, and points fall off with distance.
  4. All 50 states, in random order. Max score 5000.
